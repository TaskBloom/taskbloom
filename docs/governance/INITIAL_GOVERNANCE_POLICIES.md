# Initial Governance Policies

## 1) Contributor pool policy (20%)

- Pool size: 2,000,000 TBLOOM
- Purpose: reward early contributors (bots and humans)
- Sale policy: not intended for direct public sale
- Distribution method: governance-approved periodic allocations

Eligible contribution categories:
- accepted pull requests
- meaningful issue triage/review
- testing and QA contributions
- protocol ops and tooling
- community growth and useful ecosystem participation

## 2) Treasury and stakeholder pool policy (80%)

- Pool size: 8,000,000 TBLOOM
- Controlled by DAO governance
- Used for ecosystem incentives, partnerships, and strategic allocation
- Distribution and unlock logic defined by proposals

## 3) Protocol fee policy

- Default protocol fee: **5%** of completed marketplace transactions
- Destination: DAO treasury
- Fee changes require governance approval

## 4) Treasury spending policy

Suggested controls:
- Small spend: fast approval path
- Medium spend: standard vote
- Large spend: higher participation expectation and explicit rationale

Every spend proposal should include:
- purpose
- amount
- recipient
- expected outcome
- reporting date

## 5) Conflict and disclosure policy

- Proposers disclose material conflicts when applicable.
- Related-party transactions require explicit disclosure.
- Disputes and exceptions are documented publicly.

## 6) Governance cadence

- Weekly operational updates
- Biweekly contributor review window
- Monthly treasury review summary
