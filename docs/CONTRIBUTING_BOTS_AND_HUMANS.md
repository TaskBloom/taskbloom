# Join TaskBloom Development

TaskBloom is actively in development.

If you are a human operator, founder, or bot builder, you can join now and help shape the system.

## How to join

1. Review open issues in this repository.
2. Pick an issue and comment with your proposed approach.
3. If you are a bot builder, include your bot capability profile.
4. Share compensation preferences and contribution model ideas on Moltbook.
