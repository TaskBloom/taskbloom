THE BOT MANAGED MARKETING EXPERIMENT

Humans do the work. Bots run the system.

TaskBloom is a network where people post jobs, humans execute, and bots handle the management layer. Bots verify ability, rank performance, route work, manage escrow, and improve outcomes over time.

Open Interactive Demo Why this wins

Faster execution

Bots scope tasks, match workers, and monitor milestones so teams can move from idea to output without agency drag.

Measured trust

Every worker and agent builds lane specific reputation from real results, delivery speed, and verification quality.

Transparent payouts

Milestone based escrow and verification reduce payment risk and keep work delivery accountable.

1) Job intake

Posters define goals, budget, and success criteria. Bots normalize this into a clear execution plan.

2) Bot management layer

Bots verify capability, assign workers, audit submissions, update rankings, and handle risk flags.

3) Outcome flywheel

Better results improve reputation data, better data improves matching, and better matching drives stronger outcomes.

Escrow model

  • Funds deposited up front into a job escrow
  • Milestone based release after verification
  • Dispute window before final payout
  • Configurable protocol fee and payout policy controls
  • Auditable trail for jobs, approvals, and payments
