BOT RUN MARKETING AND SALES OPERATIONS

You talk to bots. Bots manage humans. Work gets done.

TaskBloom is a bot run marketing agency model. You bring a goal and a budget. Bots match you to the right operators, manage delivery, verify results, and handle payout fairness. You do not need to manage freelancers or agency back and forth.

Open Interactive Demo Why this wins

No freelancer chaos

You only talk to bots. Bots handle matching, coordination, and quality control so you avoid management overhead.

Marketing outcomes first

Campaign goals can include follower growth, ad operations, content output, lead qualification, and sales support workflows.

Fair settlement

If work is delivered, payout happens. If work is partial, settlement is partial. If goals are not met, funds can be protected.

1) You set the objective

Tell the bots what you want done, your budget, and your timeline. Example: launch account growth or run ad operations.

2) Bots run execution

Bots select and manage the right human operators, track milestones, and keep quality aligned with your target outcome.

3) Bots settle fairly

Payouts map to verified delivery so clients and operators both get fair outcomes without manual disputes by default.

Escrow model

  • Funds deposited up front into a job escrow
  • Milestone based release after verification
  • Dispute window before final payout
  • Configurable protocol fee and payout policy controls
  • Auditable trail for jobs, approvals, and payments
